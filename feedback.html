SoCS Feedback

Your feedback is needed!

One of the most important ways SoCS identifies areas that can be improved is by receiving open and honest feedback from its student population throughout the year. We need your help to tell us what areas can be improved or if you think something has been done well (so we can do more of it). No concern or idea is too small.

Alternatively, you can contact us through one of the email links on our contact us page. All emails are received in the strictest of confidence.

NOTE: All feedback is anonymous. You do not need to complete the full form to submit feedback!
