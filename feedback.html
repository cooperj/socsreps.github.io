SoCS FEEDBACK

We Need Your Feedback

One of the most important ways SoCS identifies areas that can be improved is by receiving open and honest feedback from its student population throughout the year. We need your help to tell us what areas can be improved or if you think something has been done well (so we can do more of it). No concern or idea is too small.

This Google Forms facility is a shared service that all university school reps have access to. This means that all responses can be read by any school rep across the university and by anyone with an '@lincolnsu.com' email. If you are not happy with this, you can contact any one of us through the email links on our Contact Us page. All emails are received in the strictest of confidence.

NOTE: All feedback is anonymous. No personal information is needed to provide feedback.

On the 11th February 2020 an email was sent to all SoCS students detailing the changes that were being made to the school with respect to Kevin & Lucs role changes. This involved the Head of CoS acting as Interim Head of School with three directors in the College overseeing the general function and management of the school. Over the last few months a new Acting Head of School has recently been appointed, Dr Salah Al-Majeed. On the 7th August we requested that Salah send out a formal introduction to help introduce himself to the current student cohort which was done on the 10th August. It was noted by SoCS that the current organizational structure is confusing; to help with this we have asked for more clarification on who is who before the new academic year begins.
