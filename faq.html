SoCS FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Throughout the academic year, the rep team get asked questions that frequently have the same answer!

To help with this we have put together some FAQs. If you want to add an FAQ please contact any one of us through the email links on our contact us page or head over to our GitHub repo and make a pull request or raise an issue.

All emails are received in the strictest of confidence.

How do I apply for an assignment extension?

Extension forms are on Blackboard under the following panel: Blackboard > My Bb Sites > School of Computer Science (At the bottom) > Useful Documents (In the panel on the left). The very top panel has the process and required forms to complete an extension request.

All extensions requests need to be handed in at least 3 days before the assignment deadline to ensure the extensions officer will see the request on time!

Be mindful, that all extension requests require comments to be evidenced, so images, screen shots etc may be required.

What are the best ways to contact my course rep?

There are several ways to contact your course reps! The best way is via email.

All course rep emails can be found on the Contact Us section of the home page.

Alternatively, your respective rep's contact details can be found on the SU Website.

Can I contact a rep anonymously?

Yes!

There are many times when you may want to raise an issue in a anonymous way. Our feedback form (Accessible here or on the "Feedback" tab along the top) does not require you to enter contact details!

Just fill out as many or as little sections as you want, then submit.
