Student Election Results!

By Garry Clawson

March 19, 2021

The Lincoln SU votes have now all been counted and the results are in!

It is with great pleasure to announce that William Oldham has been voted the new School of Computer Science School Rep for the 2021/2022 academic year! Congrats William - you will be an awesome representative for the students in SoCS!

For a full break down of all the results head over to the Lincoln SU results page where you can see a full breakdown of who has been successful in each of the roles.

Remember, we are here to help. If you are not having the experience you want then please reach out and let us know. You can contact any one of us through our email links or anonymously using our Feedback page. All emails are received in the strictest of confidence. No concern or idea is too small.
