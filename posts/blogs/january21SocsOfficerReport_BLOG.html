January SoCS Officer Report

By Garry Clawson

January 20, 2021

As we start the new 2021 year I hope that everyone had a really positive break and found some time to relax, laugh and catch up on some reading! This next part of Semester A forms the final 3rd of the term and upcoming exam period and I hope that each of you is in a position to really excel at that. Remember, if there is ever anything you feel that can be done to improve your experiences or academic output please always let your reps know as there are loads of great resources available!

This months report covers the Christmas period and of the new Year. There are several SU meetings arranged to review key concerns that students have raised over the Christmas period and I will report on these shortly. These SoS Reps reports are all freely available and can be found at www.lincolnsu.com/officer-reports.

For added transparency I will also publish the submitted reports each month as a post, this is linked here as well as shown below. We would love to hear any Feedback and remember no concern or idea is too small.
