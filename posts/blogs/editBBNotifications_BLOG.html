Editing BB Notifications

By Garry Clawson

February 14, 2021

Blackboard gives you a very easy way to stay on track of notifications via email but these are not on by default. This quick guide shows you how to update these email notification settings.

Step 1: Go to the top right and click on your name. Then click 'Edit Notification Setting'.

Step 2: Then find the respective course you wish to have email notification on, and select it.

Step 3: You will see a series of settings, just click the 'Email' checkbook for the notifications you want to receive.

That's it! It is very easy and means you don't need to check Blackboard every 10 minutes!
