Useful Online Resources

By Garry Clawson

August 12, 2020

Whether starting out at University or even if you have been here for a few years, it can be hard to find those useful resources that can help you through your course. There is not one single place for this (but always do checkout the Module recommended reading lists!) on the SoCS website so here is collection that many current first years as well as second and third have said not only found useful but also interesting.

The links are in no order and by no means complete, so if you see something that would be useful then please head over to our GitHub repo and make a pull request or raise an issue. Alternatively you can contact any one of us through our email or anonymously by using our Feedback page. All emails are received in the strictest of confidence.
