October SoCS Officer Report

By Garry Clawson

October 21, 2020

Believe it or not but I am nearly half way through my term as SoCS School Rep! Even though the appointment formally closes on July 1st 2021, there is the new Rep nomination process in March which creates a handover, so that point tends to act as the beginning of 'changing of the guards'. It goes to show how little time you really have to make an impact!

These reports are required by the 3rd Wednesday of every month (this month that was the 21st) and publication on the blog coincides with that of the LincolnSU website. It is the duty of the SoCS School Rep to provide a report to the student union about what has been achieved in the month, what wins the reps have had and what the plans are for the following month. These reports are all freely available and can be found at www.lincolnsu.com/officer-reports.

For added transparency I will also publish the submitted reports each month as a post, the October 2020 report is linked here as well as shown below. We would love to hear any Feedback and remember no concern or idea is too small.
