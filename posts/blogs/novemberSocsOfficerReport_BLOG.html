November SoCS Officer Report

By Garry Clawson

November 30, 2020

November has been a busy month with everyone getting into the groove of how online teaching and learning works and lots of ideas of how to make improvements. As always, it is the duty of the SoCS School Rep to provide a report to the student union about what has been achieved in the month, what wins the reps have had and what the plans are for the following month. These reports are submitted by the 3rd week of every month. This month it was the 25th November. These reports are all freely available and can be found at www.lincolnsu.com/officer-reports.

For added transparency I will also publish the submitted reports each month as a post, the September 2020 report is linked here as well as shown below. We would love to hear any Feedback and remember no concern or idea is too small.
