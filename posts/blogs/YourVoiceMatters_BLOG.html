Your Voice Matters

By Garry Clawson

June 18, 2020

To make sure we have the most up to date feedback as possible we will each month take a short survey that you can complete at any time through that month. We will use the ongoing feedback to help us inform the SoCS leadership team what areas are going well and what areas need improvement.

We want to make sure that only the most relevant questions are being asked so that we can work with the School to make the most impactful changes. If you wish to make a post, challenge an item, raise a question or make a comment please head over to our GitHub repo and make a pull request or raise an issue. Alternatively you can contact any one of us through our email or anonymously by using our Feedback page. All emails are received in the strictest of confidence.
