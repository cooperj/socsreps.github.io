About Us

We are the SoCS SU reps team for the academic year 20/21. We have created this blog to help with openness and transparency of the SU process as well as having a single place to show all the things that the SU reps get up to. The aim of the school and course reps is to ensure that your student voice is being heard. No idea or concern is too small. If you wish to make a post, challenge an item, raise a question or make a comment please head over to our GitHub repo and make a pull request or raise an issue. Alternatively you can contact any one of us through our email links below or anonymously using our Feedback page. All emails are received in the strictest of confidence.

How the Team Works Together

Not knowing where you fit in an organization, even as a volunteer, can make you feel a little lost or even a little anxious. A good way to remove this is by having a look at the organisation chart. Lincoln SU don't have a formal organisation chart at this level so we created one. We have included details about how often we meet with the SoCS leadership team as well as who we would generally work with through the month. No meetings are secret and we attend them on your behalf. Please ask us about them. No idea or concern is too small.
