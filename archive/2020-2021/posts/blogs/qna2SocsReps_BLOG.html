SoCS Questions & Answers 2

By Garry Clawson

October 19, 2020

There was a great response to the SoCS Questions & Answers 1 video blog, so Chris Headleand, Director of Teaching and Learning for the School of Computer Science, and I, decided to continue and develop a Series of Question and Answer sessions through the academic year to help get your questions answered directly, but also to show that questions are being presented.

These sessions are also here to help students engage with the process as the School is only as good as its student population. The more informed and questioning the students are the better position the school is in to be the best they can be for their cohort.

The forums are always a great way to share information and thanks to Chris for taking the time to answer and provide his thoughtful feedback. The video is below and if you have some time, take a look, and also feel free to get in touch and let us know of anything you want discussed. No concern or idea is too small.
