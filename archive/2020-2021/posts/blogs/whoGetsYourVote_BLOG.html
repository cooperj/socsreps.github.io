Who Gets Your Vote!

By Garry Clawson

March 15, 2021

Student volunteer officer elections upon us and will be open from the 20:00 on the 15th March 2021! The students who are fortunate enough to hold these positions can make a real and tangible difference so make sure you have your say and vote! You can find all the information you need at the Lincoln SU nominations website.

The process takes place in two sequential parts - Nominations and Voting:

  1. Nominations: 1st - 12th March - This is where a student can nominate themselves to a role. This requires that a student complete the nomination process and put a few words together to create a very high level manifesto. Some roles have limited numbers, such as there is only 1 x School Rep per School. However, roles such as course reps can have more open positions. Nominations open on the 1st March at 09:00 and close on the 12th March at 14:00.
  2. Voting: 15th - 19th March - Once nominations have closed then the voting process can begin. This is where students are invited to nominate for their preferred candidate for each of the role. Voting opens on the 15th March at 20:00 and close on the 19th March at 14:00.

Please spend just 2 minutes and Vote Here!

Remember, we are here to help. If you are not having the experience you want then please reach out and let us know. You can contact any one of us through our email links or anonymously using our Feedback page. All emails are received in the strictest of confidence. No concern or idea is too small.
