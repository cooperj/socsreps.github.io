SoE & SoCS MERGE PROPOSAL - UPDATE 1

By Garry Clawson (SoCS School Rep)

March 30, 2021

Proposal to merge the School of Engineering and School of Computer Science - Update 1

Today I have followed up on the reassurances offered by the College of Science leadership team to request further information on the proposed merger between the School of Computer Science and the School of Engineering. I hold concerns about this proposal primarily because no information or detail about it has been shared.

The School of Computer Science is one that we all know and love. Changes that have the potential to have such a large impact like a merger between two schools should be made transparent and community inclusive. For openness and transparency my email correspondence can be seen below

Email following up on proposed merge:


I ask that all members of the community to make your thoughts, comments and concerns known. Please do not be silent on this issue. If you want to do this anonymously the reps provide an anonymous feedback form that can be used. A link to this post will also be made in the SoCS Common Room on Teams that we all have access to where comments can also be made.

All question posed and any provided responses will be made through new clearly marked blog posts on this reps website. Announcements will be made to the community when this occurs.
