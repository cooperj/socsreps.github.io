SoCS & SoE Merger Questionnaire Response

By Garry Clawson

June 10, 2021

As your reps we are continually pressing the matter of the proposed merger between the SoE and SoCS. Very little, to almost no formal information has been made available about this which has left every student in the dark.

To try to gather input on the proposed SoCS & SoE merger, students were sent a questionnaire with five questions. This was sent on the 19th May 2021 at 09:43AM. The five questions were developed by the respective school reps - Patrick Carty and Garry Clawson - to gather as much input as possible at this stage on student views of this plan.

On the 11th June 2021 we will be presenting the results of this questionnaire to the CoS, who will also be hearing views and taking input from other groups (i.e. lecturers, researchers etc) about the merger.

So that we can ensure you always have as much information as we do, and that you are always kept as up to date as possible, the slides being presented at the meeting can be seen here.

Additionally and for added transparency you can see the SoCS and SoE merger student questionnaire responses here. This is in a .csv format and will show in your browser window.

If you have 10 minutes we urge all students to take some time and read through these responses and look at the slides as this is our (School reps), interpretation of these in a bullet form presentation. If there is something missing or a view not shown please contact us so we can ensure everyone has a chance to share their thoughts.

You can contact any one of us through our email links or anonymously using our Feedback page. All emails are received in the strictest of confidence. No concern or idea is too small.
