Course Rep Elections

By Garry Clawson

November 1, 2020

There are many ways to get engaged with the School of Computer Science, this can be from running a society, holding events, becoming a peer mentor or just being a friendly face to other students. However, one way that really helps is becoming a Course Rep. Course Reps are integral to how the School finds out and makes changes to better its courses but also to better the student experience. It can only do that if there is as much feedback as possible across all year groups!

Voting for new course reps opens at 9:00 AM 2nd November until the 9:00 AM 5th November.To ensure everyones experience is taken into account it is important that as many people vote as possible for the course reps candidates and to ensure that you're voice is being heard! The Course Reps are here to represent you. Please spend just 2 minutes and Vote Here!
